What we do

Site Site visit

If necessary, we will arrange a site visit so we can provide an accurate quote.

Survey

If our quote is approved we will arrange to visit the site or property to carry out a 3D “point cloud” scan. This will record every millimetre of the site in a 3D model.

Photogrammetry

In parallel with the point cloud scan our scanner can also take panoramic photos from each of the scan locations. Although not as mm accurate as the point cloud scan, these photos will be fully scalable and viewable via an online portal, rather like Google Streetview.

Registration and translation

Back at the office, we will download all of the separate scans that we recorded on site and merge them into a single point cloud, which can then be imported into our BIM software. Similarly, the panoramic photos will be stitched together and uploaded to the online viewing portal.

Modelling

We will “3D trace” the point cloud data, in doing so building an as-existing BIM model of the site/property.

Drawings

If needed, we can publish a full suite of survey drawings from the model.

Handover

Once complete, we will issue the BIM model, together with the drawings. If we are appointed as Architect we will quickly adapt the existing BIM model into a proposed concept one, ready for further development and refinement.
