Sober Strick


PARTNER


Sober Strick has been with Lees Associates since 2003 and became a partner in 2011 when founder John Lees retired. He specialises in prime listed and new-build residential projects, both in London and further afield.




Sober Strick always takes a leading role in projects. Clients appreciate his thorough, precise and disciplined nature. He has a proven track record of successfully delivering prime residential projects, both as designer and Executive Architect.




    	



		

			

			
Anberi Dose


PARTNER


Anberi has spent her career at Lees Associates, running high-end projects in the residential, retail, hotel and office sectors. She particularly values the way in which experiences in one sector can inform those in others.




She has an excellent record for retaining clients and has worked for Harrods and Selfridges for a combined period of over twenty years, with projects including the restoration of both of their facades.
